⇒ ε = 2 · (1 · 1) · 0.5 · sin(0.5t)
⇒ ε = 1 · sin(0.5t)
 ε = BAωsin(ωt)
 ε → Generated electromotive force (EMF, Volts)
B → Magnetic Field (Tesla)
A → length of coil · width of coil (m2)
ω → Angular velocity (rad/s)
The above describe the motion and generation of an ideal AC generator.
